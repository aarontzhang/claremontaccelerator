## Why We're Starting This Blog

The Claremont Accelerator has always been about more than just funding — it's about building a community of ambitious student founders across the 5Cs. This blog is our way of sharing that community with the world.

Here you'll find:

- **Founder spotlights** — stories from startups we've supported
- **Program updates** — news about cohorts, applications, and events
- **Insights** — lessons learned from mentors, VCs, and founders

## What's Coming

We have a lot in the pipeline. Expect posts from founders in Cohort 4, recaps of our Demo Day, and advice from our mentor network.

Stay tuned!
