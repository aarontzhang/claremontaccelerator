Flash Biometrics is the leading facial recognition platform built to verify identities in under a second. Attendees check in to events using their face — no tickets, no scanning, no long lines — while the system handles thousands of simultaneous identities.

Built with a privacy-first approach: biometric data is never sold or shared, and is automatically deleted after each use. Flash Biometrics powers check-in at sporting events, concerts, stadiums, and corporate functions.
