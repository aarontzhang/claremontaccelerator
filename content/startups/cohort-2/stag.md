Stag (also known as Credit OS) is an AI-powered platform designed for hedge fund treasury management. It consolidates data from multiple prime brokers, automates cash allocation, and forecasts trading costs — reducing the need for dedicated operations staff while delivering meaningful financing savings.

The "Credit OS" framing positions the product as an operating system for credit operations within institutional finance.
