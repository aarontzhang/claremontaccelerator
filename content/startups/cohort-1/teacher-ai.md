teacher.ai is an edtech startup building an AI teaching assistant toolkit designed to reduce workload for professors while improving student learning outcomes. The AI T.A. handles routine course tasks, freeing instructors to focus on higher-value interactions with students.

The team launched a pilot program at Harvey Mudd College, drawing on firsthand experience with the bottlenecks in college-level instruction.
